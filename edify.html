Edify

Edify Case Study

Edify is my main UX/UI case study that I designed as part of my CareerFoundry course. Edify is an expert app, that allow its users to connect, through video calls, with an expert in almost any field.

Full Case Study

Overview

Edify is a mobile application designed to facilitate a connection between a user and an expert. At its core Edify aims to create an environment where its users feel they may acquire knowledge that will improve their lives, whether they are seeking advice for their current profession or personal growth within in a hobby or pass time.Edify aims to create a platform that fosters trust between its users and experts, ultimately so they can learn more, learn better and learn smarter through Edify.
